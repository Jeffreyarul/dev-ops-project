7.6 Limitations and Constraints

Study Limitations
  • Scope Limitation: Testing conducted in controlled laboratory environment; real-world clinical validation pending
  • Hardware Constraints: Limited access to commercial medical devices restricted testing scope
  • Time Constraints: 6-month study period limited long-term reliability testing
  • Scale Constraints: Maximum 50 concurrent nodes tested; scalability to 1000+ nodes requires further validation
  • Attack Vector Coverage: Primary focus on network-layer attacks; physical security attacks partially addressed

7.7 Research Contributions

Technical Contributions
  • Novel Security Framework: Integrated ECC authentication with AI-driven IDS
  • Energy Optimization: 40% reduction in cryptographic overhead vs. traditional methods
